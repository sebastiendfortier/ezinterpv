Before building:
- select the revision of the compiler; example:
    . s.ssmuse.dot /ssm/net/hpcs/201402/02/base /ssm/net/hpcs/201402/02/intel13sp1u2

- select the revision of vgrid; example:
    . s.ssmuse.dot /ssm/net/cmdn/vgrid/5.3.1/${COMP_ARCH}

- make librmn available:
    . s.ssmuse.dot /ssm/net/rpn/libs/15.2
